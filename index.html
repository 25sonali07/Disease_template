Diabetes Disease

The Diabetes Disease Self-Checker is an interactive clinical assessment tool that will assist individuals ages 13 and older, and parents and caregivers of children ages 2 to 12 on deciding when to seek testing or medical care.
The online, mobile-friendly tool asks a series of questions, and based on the user’s responses, provides recommended actions and resources.
